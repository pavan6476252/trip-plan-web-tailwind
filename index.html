Top Places to Visit

Araku

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Eastern Ghats in this picturesque valley in Andhra Pradesh.

Goa

Relax on the sandy beaches, party all night long, and indulge in delicious seafood in this coastal paradise.

Jaipur

Explore the rich culture and history of the Pink City, with its magnificent forts, palaces, and museums.

Kerala

Discover the natural beauty and cultural richness of God's Own Country, with its serene backwaters, lush green hills, and vibrant festivals.

Ladakh

Experience the rugged beauty and adventure of the Himalayas, with its stunning landscapes, unique culture, and thrilling activities.

Manali

Escape to the tranquil beauty of the Himalayas, with its snow-capped peaks, glistening rivers, and picturesque valleys.

Munnar

Savor the refreshing beauty and flavor of tea in this idyllic hill station in Kerala, with its rolling hills, lush green plantations, and charming cottages.

Ooty

Experience the serene beauty and colonial charm of this quaint hill station in Tamil Nadu, with its misty hills, pristine lakes, and charming gardens.

Shimla

Immerse yourself in the enchanting beauty and colonial legacy of the Queen of Hills, with its majestic mountains, charming streets, and cozy cafes.
